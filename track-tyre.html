Guide

How to Choose Your Track Tyre

01

Know Your Compound

Soft compounds grip better but wear faster. Use SC1 for short sprints, SC2 for endurance races.

02

Match Your Bike

Check your bike's recommended tyre size. Using the wrong width affects handling and safety.

03

Warm Up Properly

Track tyres need 2–3 warm-up laps to reach optimal operating temperature. Never push cold.

04

Check Pressure

Run 1.8–2.0 bar front, 1.8–2.1 bar rear for track use. Check after every session.
